Backup & restore

Export your clients and sessions to a backup file. Import it into any new version of the app to restore your data.

How to update the app
1.Open PT Tracker → Settings → Export backup → Save to Files → Business folder
2.Delete the PT Tracker icon from your home screen
3.Upload new pt-tracker.html to Netlify, text the link to your phone
4.Tap the link → open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen
5.Open new icon → Settings → Import backup → find your .json file
✓Done — your clients and sessions are restored
